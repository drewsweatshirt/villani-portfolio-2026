Overview

Project Summary

Objective

Design and build a functional rover chassis that satisfies competition limits while remaining printable, lightweight, and structurally useful.

Tools Used

Fusion 360, CAD iteration, prototyping, structural thinking, and design-for-manufacturing decisions.

Main Focus

Balancing stiffness, weight, packaging, printability, and competition-oriented performance.
